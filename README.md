# Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance and customer behavior to identify key revenue drivers, evaluate category and product performance, assess regional sales trends, and uncover opportunities to improve profitability and business growth through data-driven decision-making.
# Business Questions
1. What are the overall sales, profit, and profit margin achieved by the business?
2. Which product categories generate the highest and lowest revenue?
3. How did category performance change between 2019 and 2020?
4. Which sub-categories contribute the most and least to total sales?
5. What factors drive higher sales performance: quantity sold, number of orders, customer count, or average order value?
6. Which regions and states contribute the most and least revenue?
7. What are the preferred payment methods and shipping modes among customers?
8. How do customer purchasing behaviors influence sales performance and profitability?
