# Project Description
This Power BI project analyzes retail sales performance across categories, products, regions, customers, and shipping methods. The dashboard provides insights into revenue drivers, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and regional performance. The analysis helps identify top-performing categories, high-revenue products, customer preferences
# Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance and customer behavior to identify key revenue drivers, evaluate category and product performance, assess regional sales trends, and uncover opportunities to improve profitability and business growth through data-driven decision-making.
# Business Questions
1. What are the overall sales, profit, and profit margin achieved by the business?
2. Which product categories generate the highest and lowest revenue?
3. How did category performance change between 2019 and 2020?
4. Which sub-categories contribute the most and least to total sales?
5. What factors drive higher sales performance: quantity sold, number of orders, customer count, or average order value?
6. Which regions and states contribute the most and least revenue?
7. What are the preferred payment methods and shipping modes among customers?
8. How do customer purchasing behaviors influence sales performance and profitability?

# Insights
* Total Sales reached $1.57M, generating $175.26K in profit with an overall Profit Margin of 11.19%.
* Office Supplies was the highest-performing category, contributing $644K in sales and approximately 41% of total revenue.
* Category leadership shifted over time: Furniture led sales in 2019, while Office Supplies became the top-performing category in 2020.
* Phones emerged as the top-performing sub-category overall, generating $197K in sales and maintaining strong performance across multiple periods.
* Sub-category performance changed significantly over time, with Chairs leading sales in 2019 and Binders becoming the top-selling sub-category in 2020.
* Revenue was highly concentrated in the West region, which generated $522K in sales, while the South region recorded the lowest sales performance.
* California was the strongest-performing state, contributing $335K in revenue and acting as the primary revenue driver for the business.
* Cash on Delivery (COD) was the most preferred payment method, accounting for more than 42% of total sales, highlighting customer preference for payment upon delivery.
* Standard Class was the dominant shipping mode, suggesting that customers prioritize cost-effective delivery options over faster shipping services.
* The business processed approximately 3K orders and sold 22K units.
* Despite strong sales growth and category performance, the overall Profit Margin of 11.19% indicates potential opportunities to improve profitability through pricing optimization and cost management.
# Conclusion
- Office Supplies emerged as the primary revenue driver, contributing over 40% of total sales.
- Technology products generated higher customer spending per order compared to Furniture.
- Phones consistently remained one of the strongest-performing sub-categories.
- Revenue was concentrated in the West region, particularly California.
- Customers showed a strong preference for COD payments and Standard Class shipping.
- While the business generated strong sales and profit, the overall profit margin suggests opportunities for further profitability improvement.
